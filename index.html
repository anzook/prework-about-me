Why do you want to be a web developer?

I've always been into computers. In middle school, I discovered the joy of building your own computer and learned all about hardware, however I never branched out to software development. I went to school to study engineering, but it was all traditional physical disciplines. I understood that well, but the more I expereinced working, the more I saw a need to interface with a digital world. My college roomate went on to do a boot camp himself, and loved it so much he stayed to teach for a bit. Finally, when I hit a turning point in my career, I realized it was time for me to learn to code as well. I'm excited for the challenges and opportunities this will provide me!
